What Does a Fox Say?

An Exclusive Insite Into The Words of a Fox

There are some that have no idea on what a fox actally sounds like.
A Song by Yvis gives us a little insite into this strange creature.

These are some of the sounds a fox can make according to Yvis

  1. "Jacha-chacha-chacha-chow"
  2. "Fraka-kaka-kaka-kaka-kow"
  3. "A-hee-ahee ha-hee"

Near the end of this magical song a fox comes in and stands on its hind legs and starts singing the following noises:
1."Wa-wa-way-do, wub-wid-bid-dum-way-do, wa-wa-way-do"
2."Bay-budabud-dum-bam"
3."Abay-ba-da bum-bum bay-do"
